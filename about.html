Key Problems

Pregnancy safety involves addressing several key problems, including:

  • Maternal health risks
  • Access to quality healthcare
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Social and economic disparities
  • Mental health concerns

Mission

At Her Health, our mission is to:

  • Ensure the safety and well-being of expectant mothers
  • Provide accessible and quality healthcare services
  • Educate and empower women about prenatal care
  • Promote mental health awareness during pregnancy
  • Address social and economic barriers to maternal health
